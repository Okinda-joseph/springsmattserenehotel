Heritage & serene confort

Discover a rich cultural heritage of Masai Mara

Nestled in the heart of a historically rich landscape, Springsmatt Serene Hotel offers a unique blend of heritage charm and modern luxury. Housed in a beautifully restored 19th-century building, the hotel retains much of its original architectural elegance, seamlessly integrating historical elements with contemporary comforts.

 

Each room is thoughtfully decorated with period furnishings, vintage art pieces, and timeless decor, allowing guests to step back in time while enjoying modern amenities. Our commitment to preserving and celebrating local heritage extends beyond the architecture, offering a variety of cultural experiences to immerse guests in the rich traditions and stories of the area.

 

Guests can enjoy guided tours of nearby historical sites, interactive workshops with local artisans, and curated exhibits showcasing the region's history and craftsmanship. Dining at Springsmatt Serene Hotel is a journey through the culinary traditions of the region, with our restaurant serving a delectable array of dishes inspired by historical recipes and local ingredients, crafted with care to highlight the flavors and techniques passed down through generations.

 

 

Set amidst lush gardens and serene landscapes, the hotel offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, with beautifully manicured gardens, heritage trails, and a picturesque spring that inspired our name, providing an ideal destination for relaxation and a deeper connection with nature.

 

We pride ourselves on providing personalized service that reflects the warmth and hospitality of the region, with our dedicated staff ensuring a comfortable and memorable stay, offering insights into local heritage and assisting with special requests.

 

The historic charm and picturesque setting make Springsmatt Serene Hotel an ideal venue for special events and celebrations, with our unique event spaces and bespoke event planning services catering to weddings, family reunions, and corporate retreats.

 

Our commitment to sustainability is evident in our eco-friendly practices, from sourcing local and organic ingredients to utilizing energy-efficient technologies, aiming to preserve the natural beauty and historical significance of our surroundings for future generations.

 

Springsmatt Serene Hotel is not just a place to stay, but a destination that offers a rich tapestry of historical and cultural experiences, inviting guests to explore the heritage of our region, relax in a serene environment, and create lasting memories.
