Garden suite

Discover garden suite available

Our three 50 sq.m perennially popular Garden Suites promise an enchanting oasis in the very heart of the city. Each Suite opens on to its own private garden terrace. As well as a sunbathing area with sunbed. Each private garden also features a covered pergola, which serves as a Mediterranean-styled private dining area. A private gate opens on to the tropical gardens of the hotel.

 

The ideal venue for a romantic weekend, the Suites also provide the optimum executive base for those wishing to conduct business in khwisero kakamega county.Each room offers, a bedroom with king-sized bed, a spacious seating area with writing desk and a lavishly appointed bathroom with bath and shower. All our guests have access to the executive lounge.

When it comes to hotel booking cancellation, the guests are required notify the hotel at least 20 to 24 hours prior to the scheduled check-in time to avoid any charges. Cancellations made within 20 to 24 hours of the reservation will incur a one-night room charge.
