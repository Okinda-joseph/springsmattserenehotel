About us

Springsmatt serene hotel rooms and suites are a harmonious blend of historical charm and modern sophistication. Each space is thoughtfully designed to provide the utmost comfort, featuring period furnishings, vintage decor, and all the contemporary amenities needed for a restful stay. Whether you choose a cozy room or an expensive suite, you’ll find a perfect retreat that reflects the timeless elegance of Springsmatt. Dining at Springsmatt Serene Hotel is an experience in itself. Our restaurant offers a delectable array of dishes inspired by traditional recipes and crafted with locally sourced ingredients. From hearty breakfasts to elegant dinners, our culinary team is dedicated to providing a dining experience that celebrates the rich flavors and heritage of the region. Enjoy your meals in an ambiance that combines historical charm with refined elegance.
